Fire Plan

In the event of a fire (or any situation that requires the evacuation of the site) all blocks will sound a fire alarm, when this happens, we expect everyone to follow the standard procedure -

  • Drop what you are doing
  • If you are in a block, proceed down the stairs and do not use the hoists/lifts
  • If you are a supervisor or are responsible for any operatives make sure they are aware of the situation and following the correct procedure
  • Exit the site in a calm and orderly manner DO NOT RUN

    • ~There are three exits that you can use to leave the site. These are as follows: The Primary Delievery Gate - Located by Block 3 on the westside of the hoarding, The Secondary Delievery Gate - Located by Block 2 on the westside of the hoarding, The Turnstiles - Located by the Main Office on the northside of the hoarding~

  • Gather outside the site at the Firepoint (this will be the Queen's Garden) located on the northside of the site
  • Wait at said Firepoint till a manager provides further instructions

    • First Aid/Incidents

    First aid kits can be found in the Main office (as well as the subcontractor office's -these will be in block 3 and in the basement for block 2- however your best bet will be the Main office). If you are injured please do come up to the Main Office and let us know, so that we can provide you with first aid - if the injury is serious we will make sure you get to the nearest hospital to get the necessary treatment.     Canteen

    On site we have 3 Canteen's that can be used

    • The Main Canteen - Hold's 26 people
    • Canteen opposite Main - Hold's 8 people
    • Finally Canteen first door on your right (coming from turnstiles/block 4) - Hold's 8 people

    When entering the Canteen's you will notice that the tables have some tape on their surfaces, this is there to keep people more separated while everyone is eating (two people per table). You can get hot meals from the Main Canteen -however please keep in mind that you can only pay in cash so if you only have card you will need to take out money before hand- once you have your meal you can take it to one of the other canteens, or eat out in the public however if you choose to do this since youd be in a public area we ask that you are mindful and respectful as we are not the only ones using the space

The site starts at 8:00am and closes at 6:00pm & you also get an hour break each day - keep in mind that you will possibly have staggered start and finish times and your break will either be two set 30min breaks (if your work interfers with someone else's) or you will have the entire hour to take at your own discretion - to know how this all affects you please speak to one of your managers and they should be able to let you know more
